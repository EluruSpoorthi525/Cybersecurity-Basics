# CIA Triad

The CIA Triad is the foundation of information security.

## Confidentiality

Ensures that information is accessible only to authorized users.

### Example
Only HR employees can access employee salary records.

---

## Integrity

Ensures data is accurate and has not been modified without authorization.

### Example
A bank transaction should not be altered during transfer.

---

## Availability

Ensures systems and data are available whenever needed.

### Example
A hospital database should remain available 24/7.

---

## Summary

The CIA Triad helps organizations protect information through Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability.
